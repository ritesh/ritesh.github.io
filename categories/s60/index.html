My favorite S60 apps

Posted on November 29, 2009 in apps, s60, symbian

I try and use my phone for everything I can. Some of the apps that I find very useful are: fring (free): Works with gtalk, twitter, fb, msn etc. If you have one of those newfangled Nseries phones, you can use the front camera to make skype video calls (over WiFi). Nokia Messaging Email (free beta, might change in the future): The native email application on the E71 is a load of crap.
